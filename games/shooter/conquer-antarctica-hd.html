Conquer Antarctica HD

Description

Conquer Antarctica HD is an awesome battle game where you try to beat the other group of penguins using your cannon! Play against computer opponent or play head to head with your friend in two player mode! There is lots of levels with different islands with different difficulty ratings. There is 3 weapons to use, but use them wisely.

Instructions

  • Use the mouse to adjust the angle and power, then press fire
