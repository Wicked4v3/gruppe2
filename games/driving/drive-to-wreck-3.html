Drive To Wreck 3

Description

Play the third part of the destruction game Drive To Wreck. Drive an excavator equipped with a wrecking ball over mountains, sand piles and other challenging obstacles. Balance the excavator carefully all the way to avoid damaging it or flipping over. Use the excavator wrecking ball to clear the way from obstacles, destroy buildings and open the truck trailer ramp at the finish.

Instructions

  • Use arrow keys to drive and balance the excavator.
