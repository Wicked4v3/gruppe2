Cargo Drive

Description

Cargo Drive is a realistic cargo delivery simulation game featuring a stunning 3D open world truck driving experience. Work at the warehouse as truck driver and deliver various cargo to the destination through a large open world forest. Use the navigation map to find the shortest route to the unload areas and deliver the cargo in time. Complete tasks to earn cash for spending in the garage for truck upgrades.

Instructions

  • WASD/Arrow Keys to drive the truck
