Earn To Die 2012

Description

In Earn to Die 2012, your goal is to drive your way through the zombie apocalypse, smashing through hordes of zombies along the way. Unlock and upgrade cool new vehicles as you attempt to drive your way to safety. Do you have what it takes to take on a world of undead corpses? Can you survive and make out alive?!

Instructions

  • Up Arrow: Accelerate
  • Left/Right Arrows: Tilt
  • Ctrl/X: Boost
